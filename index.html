What is an evoucher?

An eVoucher is tangible medium of exchange that Customers can use to digitalize their Euros. With the amount of funds a Customer wishes to use to pay for a product or service either online or offline, the Customer can exchange their bills and coins for a printable eVoucher, which is protected by SafeCash’s proprietary encryption engine, at a Point of Sale of kiosk – located here. Once a Custmer has attained an SafeCash eVoucher, he or she can use her funds at select Merchants either online or in-store. Merchant’s businesses can be found here.
